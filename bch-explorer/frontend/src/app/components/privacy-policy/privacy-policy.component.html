Privacy Policy

Updated: April 12, 2026


For maximum privacy, we recommend that you use your own self-hosted instance of BCH Explorer on your own hardware. You can easily install your own self-hosted instance of this website on a Raspberry Pi. See our project's GitLab page for more details about self-hosting this website. By using your own self-hosted instance you will have maximum security, privacy and freedom.


The bchexplorer.cash website, its associated API services, and related network and server infrastructure (collectively, the "Website") are operated by Melroy van den Berg. It is self-hosted and is the official Bitcoin Cash explorer (bchexplorer.cash) website.

This website and its API service (collectively, the "Website") are operated by a member of the Bitcoin Cash community ("We" or "Us"). This is not the official Bitcoin Cash explorer website, for support please contact the operator of this site.


By accessing this Website, you agree to the following Privacy Policy:

USING THIS WEBSITE

Out of respect for the Bitcoin Cash community, this Website does not use any first-party cookies, except to store your preferred language and theme settings (if any). However, we do use minimal first-party analytics and logging as needed for the operation of this Website, as follows:

  • We use webserver logging (Angie) for sysadmin purposes, which collects your IP address along with the requests you make. These logs are deleted after 30 days, and we do not share this data with any third-party.

  • We use self-hosted statistics applications (Telegraf, Grafana and Fail2Ban) for analytics purposes, which collects your IP address along with the requests you make. Our Grafana instances are configured to respect your privacy by keeping this data local and by not sharing this data with any third-party.

  • We do NOT use any third-party tracking or analytics services.

External Services

We make use of one external service to support CashTokens on the Bitcoin Cash (BCH) network:

  • https://bcmr.flowee.cash — a Bitcoin Cash Metadata Registries (BCMR) indexer provided by Flowee. This service is used solely to retrieve token metadata (such as token names, icons, and other registry information) for CashTokens displayed on this explorer. No personal data is sent to this service.
